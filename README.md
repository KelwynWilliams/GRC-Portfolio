## Why Accounting → GRC?

Most GRC candidates understand frameworks in theory.
I understand them in practice because I have spent 7 years inside the control
environments they describe.

- I have prepared and reviewed SOX workpapers — not just read about them
- I have presented risk findings to CFOs and audit committees
- I understand financial statement risk because I have prepared financial statements
- I know what a real control deficiency looks like because I have documented them

GRC needs analysts who can bridge the gap between technical frameworks and
business reality. That is exactly what years in accounting builds.
